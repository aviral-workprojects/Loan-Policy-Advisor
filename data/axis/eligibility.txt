Axis Bank Personal Loan — Eligibility, Features, and Decision Logic (2026)

Sources: Axis Bank Direct, NoBroker, Paisabazaar (Feb–Mar 2026)
Data confidence: High | Last updated: March 2026

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Age: 21 to 60 years at loan maturity
Minimum monthly income: ₹15,000 (existing Axis Bank customers); ₹25,000 (new customers)
CIBIL score: 750 or above for standard approval; 700–749 considered at higher rates
Employment: Salaried (public/private/government), self-employed, professionals (doctors, CAs, lawyers)
Work experience: Minimum 12 months (salaried); 36 months business continuity (self-employed)
Salary account: Not mandatory; existing Axis customers get preferential ₹15,000 income threshold

INCOME THRESHOLD LOGIC:
New customers (no prior Axis relationship): ₹25,000/month minimum
Existing Axis Bank account holders: ₹15,000/month minimum
Tiered threshold is a key differentiator — Axis customers face a 40% lower income barrier

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
INTEREST RATES (2026)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Starting rate: 9.98% per annum (updated; prior 10.49% was outdated)
Range: 9.98% to 22% p.a.
Pricing: Repo Rate (5.25%) + spread 2.6%–2.9% for salaried
CIBIL 750+: 9.98%–12% (prime tier)
CIBIL 700–749: 13%–17% (standard tier)
CIBIL below 700: Likely rejection or rate exceeds 17%

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
LOAN FEATURES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Loan amount: ₹50,000 to ₹40 lakh
Tenure: 12 to 84 months
Processing fee: Up to 2% + GST
Collateral: None required (unsecured)
Disbursal: Within 48 hours for eligible applicants
Login fee: ₹5,000 + GST — NON-REFUNDABLE even if rejected (critical advisory point)

PREPAYMENT:
NIL charges for up to 25% of outstanding principal per year after 12 EMIs
Best-in-class prepayment terms — superior to HDFC and ICICI 12-month lock-ins
Recommended for borrowers expecting annual bonuses

Late payment penalty: 8% p.a. above applicable rate (capped at 24%)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SPECIAL PRODUCTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Pre-approved offers: Instant disbursal for existing customers with clean history
Balance transfer: Transfer high-rate loans from other banks to Axis
Top-up loans: Available after 6 months of clean repayment
Axis QuickPay: Pay higher principal early to alter amortization curve
Axis Super Saver: Interest on (Loan minus Savings Balance) — reduces effective rate

Niche home loan products:
Axis Asha Home Loan: ₹1 lakh to ₹28 lakh; min income ₹8,000/month; up to 90% LTV
Axis Shubh Aarambh: Up to ₹30 lakh; EMI waiver incentives for clean repayment
Axis Fast Forward: Minimum ₹30 lakh for mid-to-high income borrowers

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Mandatory: PAN Card, Aadhaar Card
KYC: Passport, Driving License, Voter ID (any one)
Income: Last 3 months salary slips
Banking: Last 3–6 months bank statements
Tax: Form 16 (salaried); ITR last 2 years (self-employed)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
DTI GUIDELINES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Recommended DTI: Below 40%
Hard ceiling: 50% of net monthly income
EMI example: ₹5 lakh at 11% for 36 months = ₹16,607/month EMI

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
REJECTION TRIGGERS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CIBIL below 700: Near-certain rejection
Income below threshold (₹15k existing / ₹25k new): Redirect to Axis Asha
Employment below 12 months: High rejection probability
Self-employed business proof below 36 months: Automatic rejection
DTI above 50%: Rejected
Multiple credit inquiries in last 6 months: Signals credit hunger, reduces approval

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
RECOMMENDATION LOGIC (ADVISORY SYSTEM)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

For existing Axis customers: ₹15,000 threshold — lowest of all major banks
For prepayment focus: Axis 25% annual free prepayment is best-in-class
For CIBIL 700–749: Axis more flexible than HDFC; expect rate above 13%
For self-employed: Need 3+ years ITR and CA-certified business continuity
For joint applications: Income clubbing with spouse increases maximum eligibility
Employer category: Category A employers (top MNCs, PSUs) attract better spreads
