Axis Bank Personal Loan - Eligibility and Features

Axis Bank offers personal loans ranging from ₹50,000 to ₹40 lakh.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Age: 21 to 60 years
- Minimum monthly income: ₹15,000
- CIBIL score: 720 or above
- Employment: Salaried employees, self-employed individuals, professionals
- Work experience: Minimum 1 year (12 months)
- Nationality: Indian resident

INTEREST RATES:
- Starting from 10.49% per annum
- Rate depends on credit profile, income, and employer category

LOAN FEATURES:
- Loan amount: ₹50,000 to ₹40 lakh
- Tenure: 12 to 60 months
- Processing fee: Up to 2% + GST
- No collateral or security required (unsecured loan)
- Quick disbursal within 48 hours for eligible applicants

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED:
- KYC documents (Aadhaar, PAN)
- Salary slips (last 3 months)
- Bank statements (last 6 months)
- Form 16 / ITR for self-employed
- Employment proof

SPECIAL FEATURES:
- Pre-approved offers for existing Axis customers
- Balance transfer facility available
- Top-up loans available after 6 months of repayment
- Flexible EMI options

DTI GUIDELINES:
- Recommended debt-to-income ratio below 40%
- Total EMI obligations should not exceed 50% of net monthly income
