RBI Guidelines on Personal Loans, Consumer Credit, and Digital Lending (2024–2026)

Sources: RBI Banking Regulation Act 1949, Master Circulars, Digital Lending Guidelines 2022, RBI Press Releases 2025
Updated: March 2026

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
REGULATORY FRAMEWORK
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Governing legislation: Banking Regulation Act, 1949
Key regulators: Reserve Bank of India (RBI), Banking Ombudsman
Applicable to: All scheduled commercial banks, NBFCs, and regulated lending entities

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
1. FAIR PRACTICES CODE (FPC)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Mandatory disclosures before loan disbursement:
- Interest rate (as APR — Annual Percentage Rate)
- All fees, charges, and their calculation basis
- Prepayment and foreclosure charges
- Consequences of default (late payment fees, credit bureau reporting)
- Grievance redressal mechanism

No hidden charges: All charges must be disclosed upfront in the loan sanction letter
Standardized loan agreement: All terms in writing before disbursal
Cooling-off period: 3 days after loan sanction — borrower can cancel without penalty

CRITICAL GUARDRAIL FOR ADVISORY SYSTEMS:
An AI loan advisor MUST NOT guarantee loan approval — this violates FPC.
System must state: "This is an eligibility estimate, not a loan approval guarantee."

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
2. INTEREST RATE REGULATIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Banks must use transparent benchmark rates:
- MCLR (Marginal Cost of Funds based Lending Rate) — internal benchmark
- External benchmark: Repo Rate, 3-month Treasury Bill, 6-month Treasury Bill
- All new floating rate loans must be linked to external benchmark (RBI mandate since 2019)

Key pricing rules:
- Interest on daily reducing balance basis (not monthly/annual flat rate)
- No pre-payment penalty on floating rate loans (RBI regulation)
- Fixed rate loans: Pre-payment penalty allowed (typically 2%–4% by private banks)
- APR must be disclosed and includes: interest + processing fee + all charges

Current reference rates (March 2026):
- Repo Rate: 5.25%
- SBI MCLR (2-year): 8.75%
- SBI MCLR (1-year): 8.55%

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
3. KYC COMPLIANCE (MANDATORY FOR ALL LOANS)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

All borrowers must complete KYC before loan disbursement
Accepted KYC methods:
- Physical KYC at branch: Aadhaar + one additional ID document
- e-KYC: Aadhaar OTP-based (instant, no physical presence required)
- Video KYC: For digital loans (RBI-approved since 2020); reduces processing time to 24 hours

Aadhaar e-KYC is sufficient for most banks — eliminates document courier friction
For home loans and large amounts: Full physical KYC may still be required

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
4. CREDIT BUREAU REQUIREMENTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Mandatory: All lenders must check credit bureau report before disbursing any loan
All 4 bureaus are RBI-approved: CIBIL (TransUnion), Equifax, Experian, CRIF High Mark
Best practice: RBI recommends checking all 4 bureaus (some banks check only CIBIL)
CIBIL scale: 300 to 900 (industry standard; same across all 4 bureaus)

IMPORTANT: RBI does NOT set minimum CIBIL requirements — each bank sets its own policy
This is why the same applicant may be approved at ICICI (700 floor) but rejected at Axis (750 floor)

Reporting obligation: All approved loans must be reported to credit bureaus within 30 days
Default reporting: Missed EMIs reported to credit bureaus after 30/60/90 day windows
CIBIL impact of hard inquiry: Each bank application triggers a hard inquiry (~5–10 point temporary drop)
CIBIL repair timeline: Negative events (defaults) stay on record for 7 years

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
5. DIGITAL LENDING GUIDELINES (RBI, 2022)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Loan disbursement: Only to borrower's own bank account (not to third party)
No automatic credit limit increase: Lender cannot increase limit without explicit borrower consent
APR disclosure: Mandatory — borrowers must see true cost of credit including all charges
Data privacy: All customer data collection must comply with applicable privacy laws
No third-party data sharing: Borrower data cannot be shared with unauthorized third parties
App-based lending: Lending apps must be listed on Google Play Store (RBI-registered apps only)

CRITICAL FOR AI SYSTEMS:
Digital loan advisors fall under RBI's oversight of "Lending Service Providers" (LSPs)
AI systems must:
1. Not make approval guarantees
2. Show complete rate + fee breakdown (not just headline rate)
3. Not initiate loan applications without explicit borrower consent
4. Maintain audit trail of all recommendations

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
6. RECOVERY PRACTICES (RBI CIRCULAR)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Recovery agents: Must carry official ID cards from the lender
Permitted calling hours: 8 AM to 7 PM only (no calls at night or on declared holidays)
No harassment: Physical or psychological harassment in recovery is illegal
Complaint mechanism: Borrowers can file complaint with Banking Ombudsman (free of charge)
Legal recourse: SARFAESI Act for secured loans; civil court for unsecured personal loans

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
7. CONSUMER PROTECTION RIGHTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Right to information: Banks must provide all charges before disbursement
Right to be heard: Banks must acknowledge complaints within 5 business days
Right to redressal: Banking Ombudsman handles unresolved complaints (free)
Right to fair recovery: No intimidation or harassment in collections
Right to credit report: Borrowers entitled to one free credit report per year from each bureau
Right to nominate: Borrowers can nominate a person for loan settlement in case of death

Banking Ombudsman jurisdiction:
Covers: Delays in loan processing, incorrect charges, harassment in recovery, mis-selling
File online: bankingombudsman.rbi.org.in or cms.rbi.org.in

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
8. SPECIAL REGULATORY SCHEMES AND INCENTIVES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PMAY-CLSS (Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana — Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme):
Income groups: EWS (below ₹3 lakh/year), LIG (₹3–6 lakh), MIG-I (₹6–12 lakh), MIG-II (₹12–18 lakh)
Subsidy: Up to ₹2.67 lakh for EWS; up to ₹2.35 lakh for LIG
Implemented through: SBI, HDFC, LIC Housing Finance, and other approved lenders
First-time home buyers: Priority for this subsidy

Priority sector lending (PSL) obligations:
Banks must lend 40% of ANBC to priority sectors (agriculture, MSME, education, housing)
Personal loans to low-income segments sometimes benefit from PSL classification

EV loan incentive (SBI):
0.50% rate reduction for electric vehicle loans
No foreclosure charges after 2 years on vehicle loans
Encourages green vehicle adoption

Female borrower concession:
SBI: 0.05% rate reduction when woman is primary applicant (home loan)
Benefits joint applicants — designating wife as primary applicant is standard optimization

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
9. COMPLIANCE GUARDRAILS FOR LOAN ADVISORY SYSTEMS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

MUST NOT:
- Guarantee loan approval (violates FPC)
- Recommend loan products without disclosing all applicable rates and charges
- Misrepresent bank terms or overstate loan amount eligibility
- Collect borrower data beyond what is necessary for advisory purposes
- Show only headline interest rate — must show processing fee impact on effective rate

MUST:
- Advise customers to read loan documents carefully before signing
- Disclose that recommendations are algorithmic estimates, not bank approvals
- Show alternative options (not just the "best" option)
- Warn about non-refundable fees (Axis ₹5,000 login fee) before recommending application
- Present credit score improvement pathways for rejected applicants
- Disclose if recommending an NBFC/fintech (due to higher rate implications)

ADVISORY DISCLAIMER (recommended for all AI loan systems):
"This eligibility assessment is based on the information provided and publicly available bank policies. 
It is not a guarantee of loan approval. Final eligibility and interest rates are determined solely by 
the lender based on their complete underwriting process. Rates shown are indicative and subject to 
change. Please verify current rates and terms directly with the bank before applying."
