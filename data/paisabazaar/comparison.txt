KEY MARKET HEURISTICS:
1. DTI Rule: Keep total EMI obligations below 40-50% of net monthly income
2. Credit Score Tiers:
   - 750+: Best rates, pre-approved offers
   - 700-749: Approved with standard rates
   - 650-699: Possible with conditions or higher rates
   - Below 650: Rejection likely from major banks
3. Income-to-Loan Ratio: Banks typically lend 10-20x monthly income
4. Employer Category Matters: MNCs and PSUs get better terms

ELIGIBILITY TIPS:
- Adding a co-applicant can boost eligibility
- Clearing existing EMIs before applying improves DTI
- Salary account with target bank helps approval
- Avoid multiple applications (reduces credit score)

EMI CALCULATION GUIDE:
- For ₹5 lakh @ 12% for 36 months: EMI = ₹16,607
- For ₹10 lakh @ 11% for 48 months: EMI = ₹25,850
- Golden rule: EMI should not exceed 40% of net salary
