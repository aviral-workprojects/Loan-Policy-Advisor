SBI (State Bank of India) Personal Loan - Xpress Credit

SBI is India's largest public sector bank with the widest reach.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Age: 21 to 58 years (loan must be repaid before age 58)
- Minimum monthly income: ₹15,000 (lowest threshold among major banks)
- CIBIL score: 700 or above
- Employment: Salaried (government, PSU, private sector), self-employed
- Work experience: Minimum 12 months
- Relationship with SBI: Salary account with SBI preferred but not mandatory

INTEREST RATES:
- Starting from 11.05% per annum (slightly higher than private banks)
- Government employees get preferential rates starting 11.05%
- Private sector employees: 12% to 14%
- Self-employed: 13% to 15%

LOAN FEATURES:
- Amount: ₹1,000 to ₹20 lakh
- Tenure: 6 to 72 months
- No processing fees for existing SBI customers
- Processing fee: 1.5% for new customers
- No pre-payment penalty after 6 months

DOCUMENTS:
- Aadhaar Card + PAN
- Salary certificate / Form 16
- Last 3 months salary slips
- Bank statements (last 6 months)
- SBI account statement preferred

SBI XPRESS ELITE:
- For net monthly income > ₹1 lakh
- Higher loan amounts available
- Rate starting 11.05%

ADVANTAGES:
- Lowest income threshold (₹15,000)
- Government sector employees get best rates
- Widest branch network for assistance
- Lowest age requirement broadly accessible
