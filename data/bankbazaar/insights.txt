SHADOW LOGIC — HIDDEN PRICING MECHANICS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Risk-based pricing (Repo + Spread):
Public banks: Repo Rate + MCLR spread (slower to adjust — rate stability)
Private banks: Repo Rate + internal spread (faster to adjust — better for falling rate environment)

Axis spread breakdown for personal loans:
Base: Repo Rate 5.25% + spread 2.6%–2.9% for salaried = 7.85%–8.15% base
After cost of funds and margins: effective personal loan rates 9.98%–22%

CIBIL-to-spread conversion:
CIBIL 800+: Spread of 2.5%–2.6% (prime)
CIBIL 750–799: Spread of 2.6%–3.5%
CIBIL 700–749: Spread of 4%–6%
CIBIL 650–699: Spread of 7%–12% or rejection

Employer category pricing penalty:
Category A employer: No additional spread (base rate)
Category C employer: +1%–2% above base rate
Self-employed (unverified income): +2%–4% above salaried equivalent

LTV risk trigger (home loans):
Below 80% LTV: Standard spread
80%–90% LTV: +0.25%–0.50% spread (higher bank exposure)
Above 90% LTV: Hard rejection (RBI regulatory ceiling)

FACTORS AFFECTING YOUR PERSONAL LOAN RATE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Ranked by impact (highest to lowest):

1. CIBIL Score: Primary rate determinant — 750+ mandatory for prime; each 50-point drop costs 1%–3% more
2. Income level: Higher income = lower spread; ₹1 lakh+ NMI qualifies for premium products
3. Employer reputation: Top MNC/PSU gets 0.5%–1.5% better rate vs small private company
4. Existing banking relationship: Salary account triggers pre-approved and rate waiver
5. Loan amount: Higher amounts (>₹10 lakh) often get better rates (scale discount)
6. Tenure: Shorter tenure = lower risk = marginally better rate
7. Debt obligations (DTI): Clean DTI profile gets faster approval without rate penalty

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
COMMON REJECTION REASONS AND FIXES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CIBIL below 650:
Fix: Build credit with Step-UP card (SBM Bank); 6–12 months clean usage adds 50–100 points

High existing EMI obligations (DTI >50%):
Fix: Close one existing loan before applying; reduces DTI and may shift CIBIL tier

Short employment history (<12 months):
Fix: Wait until 12-month mark; avoid applications during probation period

Multiple recent credit inquiries:
Fix: Space applications by 3–6 months; use soft-inquiry eligibility checkers before applying

Income below bank threshold:
Fix: Add co-applicant (spouse income clubbing); switch to bank with lower threshold (Axis for existing customers)

Document discrepancies:
Fix: Ensure salary slips, bank statements, and ITR all show consistent income figures
Critical ITR check: Digital signature required — if absent, submit tax challan + CPC document

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
HOW TO IMPROVE APPROVAL CHANCES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CIBIL: Maintain above 750; check report every 6 months for errors
DTI: Keep total EMIs below 35% of net income (not just 40–50% ceiling)
Timing: Do not apply within 3 months of job change or other major financial events
Debt reduction: Pay off credit card outstanding fully before applying (reduces utilisation ratio)
Single application: Apply to one bank at a time (each hard inquiry hurts CIBIL by 5–10 points)
Existing relationship: Open salary account at target bank 6 months before applying
Annual bonus strategy: Time application after bonus receipt to show higher bank balance
Document preparation: Pre-collect all documents to avoid delays that flag the application as uncertain

SEGMENT-SPECIFIC RECOMMENDATIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Low income (<₹25,000/month):
SBI Xpress Credit if government employee (₹5,000 minimum)
Axis Asha Home Loan for property financing (min ₹8,000 income)
PMAY subsidy for first-time home buyers

Mid income (₹25,000–₹75,000/month):
ICICI for fastest processing and best digital experience
Axis for best prepayment terms (25% annual free)
SBI for lowest home loan rate (7.25%)

High income (>₹75,000/month):
HDFC Golden Edge (>₹20 lakh loans, rate from 10.75%)
ICICI Insta Loan for pre-approved
Axis Super Saver home loan (interest on net outstanding)
Negotiate spread reduction — HNI borrowers have leverage

Government/Defence:
SBI Xpress Credit / Shaurya — best rates (10.00%–10.45%)
Zero processing fee for existing SBI customers
Dedicated relationship manager at SBI branches

Self-employed professionals (doctors, CAs, lawyers):
ICICI most accommodating (relaxed age and stability rules for doctors)
Axis and HDFC accept professionals but require 36 months business continuity
Annual net profit verification required (not just gross income)
Ensure ITR has digital signature — common rejection cause

First-time borrowers (NTC):
HDFC and ICICI use alternate data (bank statement analysis)
SBM Bank Step-UP secured credit card — converts NTC to thin-file in 6 months
Avoid SBI and public banks initially — heavy CIBIL dependency
