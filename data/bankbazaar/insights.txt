BankBazaar Personal Loan Insights - 2024

INTEREST RATE TRENDS:
- RBI Repo Rate: 6.50% (as of mid-2024)
- Banks typically price personal loans at Repo + 4-10%
- MCLR-linked loans for existing customers

FACTORS AFFECTING YOUR RATE:
1. CIBIL Score (most impactful) — higher score = lower rate
2. Income level — higher income = lower rate
3. Employer reputation — top-tier MNC vs small company
4. Existing banking relationship
5. Loan amount and tenure
6. Debt obligations (DTI)

COMMON REJECTION REASONS:
- CIBIL score below 650
- High existing EMI obligations
- Short employment history (<12 months)
- Multiple recent credit inquiries
- Discrepancies in documents
- Income below bank threshold

HOW TO IMPROVE APPROVAL CHANCES:
- Maintain CIBIL score above 750
- Keep DTI below 35%
- Avoid job changes before applying
- Reduce existing debt first
- Apply to one bank at a time

FINTECH vs TRADITIONAL BANKS:
- Fintechs (KreditBee, MoneyTap): Accept lower scores, higher rates 24-36%
- Traditional banks: Stricter criteria, lower rates 10-18%
- NBFCs: Middle ground 15-24%

PROCESSING FEE GUIDE:
- SBI: 1.5% (waived for existing customers)
- HDFC: Up to 2.5%
- ICICI: 0.5% to 2.5%
- Axis: Up to 2%
