HDFC Bank Personal Loan - Eligibility and Features

HDFC Bank is India's largest private bank offering competitive personal loan products.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Age: 21 to 60 years
- Minimum monthly income: ₹20,000
- CIBIL score: 750 or above (strictest among major private banks)
- Employment: Salaried, self-employed, or professionals
- Work experience: Minimum 12 months (2 years preferred)
- Debt-to-income ratio: Must not exceed 50%

INTEREST RATES:
- Starting from 10.50% per annum
- Range: 10.50% to 21% based on profile
- Special rates for HDFC salary account holders

LOAN FEATURES:
- Amount: ₹50,000 to ₹40 lakh
- Tenure: 12 to 60 months
- Processing fee: Up to 2.5% of loan amount + GST
- Foreclosure charges: 2-4% after 12 months
- 24-hour disbursal for pre-approved offers

DOCUMENTS:
- Aadhaar Card
- PAN Card
- Salary slips (last 3 months)
- Bank statements (last 3 months)
- Employment proof

UNIQUE FEATURES:
- SmartEMI option — convert purchases to EMI
- Salary account customers get pre-approved offers
- Online application with video KYC
- Highest credit score requirement in category (750+)

NOTE: HDFC requires the highest CIBIL score (750) among major banks.
DTI ratio is strictly evaluated and must be below 50%.
