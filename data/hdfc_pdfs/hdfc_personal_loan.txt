HDFC Bank Personal Loan — Eligibility, Features, and Decision Logic (2026)

Sources: HDFC Bank Direct, NoBroker, ClearTax, Loanbaba, Paisabazaar (Mar 2026)
Data confidence: High | Last updated: March 2026

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Age: 21 to 60 years at loan maturity
Minimum monthly income: ₹25,000 (updated from ₹20,000 per 2026 research)
CIBIL score: 700 floor; 750+ for prime rates; some sources cite 600 minimum (bank direct vs aggregator variance)
Employment: Salaried, self-employed, professionals, government employees
Work experience: Minimum 12 months; 24 months preferred for best rates
DTI: Must not exceed 50% of net monthly income (strictly evaluated)
Salary account: Not mandatory; HDFC salary account holders get pre-approved offers

CIBIL TIER IMPACT AT HDFC:
750+: Prime rate access (9.98%–12%); pre-approved status possible
700–749: Standard approval; rates 13%–18%
600–699: Possible with enhanced documentation; rates 18%–24%
Below 600: Rejection from standard products; New-to-Credit (NTC) applicants may use alternate data

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
INTEREST RATES (2026)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Starting rate: 9.98% per annum
Range: 9.98% to 24% p.a.
HDFC salary account holders: Special preferential rates
Golden Edge product (NMI > ₹75,000): Premium rates starting 10.75%

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
LOAN FEATURES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Loan amount: ₹50,000 to ₹75 lakh (₹25 lakh cap for digital-only applications)
Tenure: 3 to 72 months
Processing fee: 0.99% to 2.50% (cap ₹6,500 + GST for standard; may vary by product)
Foreclosure: 2%–4% of outstanding principal (after 12 EMIs minimum lock-in)
Disbursal: 24 hours for pre-approved; 10-second disbursal for select pre-approved customers
Late payment: 24% p.a. (2% per month) on overdue — highest penalty among major banks

PREPAYMENT TERMS:
Lock-in: 12 months — no prepayment in first year
After 12 months: 2%–4% of outstanding principal as charges
Less favourable than Axis (which allows 25% free annually after 12 EMIs)
Avoid recommending HDFC for borrowers expecting early repayment

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
SPECIAL PRODUCTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Golden Edge: For NMI > ₹75,000; loan amounts >₹20 lakh; premium rates from 10.75%
Diva: Women-focused product with special terms
SmartEMI: Convert large purchases to EMI at point of sale
Pre-approved: Instant disbursal including 10-second disbursals for select salary account customers
Video KYC: Fully digital application + verification available
NTC (New to Credit) pathway: Bank statement analysis and alternate data scoring — more accommodating than SBI for first-time borrowers

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Mandatory: ID Proof, Address Proof, Income Proof
KYC: Aadhaar, Voter ID, Utility Bills (any two)
Income: Last 2 months salary slips (reduced from 3 months — quicker processing)
Banking: Last 3–6 months bank statements
Tax: Latest Form 16

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
DTI GUIDELINES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Hard ceiling: 50% DTI
HDFC strictness: Among the most rigorous DTI evaluations among private banks
Recommendation: Reduce existing EMIs before applying to HDFC if close to 50% threshold

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
REJECTION TRIGGERS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CIBIL below 600: Rejection (hard floor)
CIBIL 600–699: High scrutiny; require enhanced documentation
Income below ₹25,000/month: Rejection for standard product
DTI above 50%: Rejected
Employment below 12 months: High rejection probability
Multiple recent credit inquiries: Negative signal

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
RECOMMENDATION LOGIC
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

For HNI borrowers (NMI > ₹75,000): Golden Edge product for >₹20 lakh with best rates
For first-time/NTC borrowers: HDFC uses alternate data (bank statements) — better than SBI for NTC
For prepayment-focused: Avoid HDFC; choose Axis instead (25% free annual prepayment)
For women applicants: Diva product may offer additional benefits
For large loan amounts: HDFC can go up to ₹75 lakh — highest limit among major banks
Income clubbing: Spouse income clubbing permitted to increase eligibility
