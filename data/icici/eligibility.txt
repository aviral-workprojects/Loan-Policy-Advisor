ICICI Bank Personal Loan - Complete Guide

ICICI Bank provides personal loans to salaried and self-employed individuals for various needs including travel, medical emergencies, home renovation, and debt consolidation.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Age: 23 to 58 years
- Minimum monthly income: ₹25,000 (higher than most private banks)
- CIBIL score: 700 or above (750+ for best rates)
- Employment: Salaried in MNCs, PSUs, or government; self-employed professionals
- Work experience: Minimum 24 months total; 1 year with current employer
- Employer category: A/B/C rated companies preferred

INTEREST RATES:
- Starting from 10.65% per annum
- Rates vary: 10.65% – 16% based on profile
- Pre-approved customers get preferential rates

LOAN FEATURES:
- Amount: ₹50,000 to ₹50 lakh
- Tenure: 12 to 72 months
- Processing fees: 0.5% to 2.5% + applicable taxes
- No prepayment charges after 12 EMIs
- Part-prepayment allowed

DOCUMENTS:
- PAN Card (mandatory)
- Aadhaar Card
- Last 3 months salary slips
- Last 6 months bank statements
- Employment letter / ID

ICICI INSTA PERSONAL LOAN:
- For pre-approved customers only
- Instant disbursal in minutes
- No documentation required
- Amount up to ₹25 lakh

COMPARISON NOTES:
- Higher income threshold than Axis and SBI
- Longer minimum tenure compared to some peers
- Strong digital process — fully online application possible
